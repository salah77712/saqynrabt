## 1. Introduction and Scope

SAQYN RABT ("**Company**," "**We**," "**Us**," or "**Our**") is a company registered in the **State of Qatar** and operates as the data controller for the personal information collected through our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

**This Privacy Policy is prepared in compliance with Law No. 13 of 2016 on the Protection of Personal Data and its executive regulations, as well as the Qatari Civil Code (Law No. 22 of 2004).** For international clients, this policy also respects the principles of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- Our public website at **saqynrabt.com**
- Our proprietary SaaS dashboard at **/dashboard**
- All related services, tools, and communications

**This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that we do not own or control.**

If you have any questions about this policy, you may contact us at **hello@saqynrabt.com** or our Data Protection Officer at **privacy@saqynrabt.com**.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, use our services, or communicate with us, we collect:

- **Account Information:** Full name, email address, phone number, company name, physical address, and employee names.
- **Business Content:** Company policies, standard operating procedures (SOPs), HR documents, and uploaded PDF files that you choose to ingest into our RAG system.
- **Payment Information:** We process payments through Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers — only transaction IDs and the last four digits of your payment method for reference purposes.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or use our dashboard, we automatically collect:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent on each page, feature interactions)

### 2.3 Chat Data

We collect the full text of messages you send to our AI-powered chat interface, as well as the AI's generated responses. This data is used to improve the accuracy and relevance of our RAG pipeline and to maintain a history of your interactions.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under **Law No. 13 of 2016** (Article 2 et seq.), processing of personal data must have a lawful basis. We process your data under the following bases:

- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide, maintain, and deliver the services you have subscribed to under our Terms of Service. Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract under Article 171 of the Qatari Civil Code.
- **Legitimate Interest:** To improve our platform, monitor usage, detect security threats, and generate aggregate analytics.
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable Qatari laws, including Law No. 24 of 2018 on Tax and anti-money laundering regulations.
- **Consent:** Where required for marketing communications or processing of special categories of data. You may withdraw consent at any time.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, in accordance with Qatari legal requirements.

| Data Category | Retention Period |
|---------------|------------------|
| **Documents / PDFs** | Retained for the duration of your subscription plus **30 days**, then permanently deleted. May be retained longer if required for ongoing legal disputes or regulatory investigations. |
| **Chat / Usage Logs** | Retained for a maximum of **3 years** for auditing, billing dispute resolution, and service improvement, after which they are securely anonymized or purged from NeonDB and Cloudflare R2. |

## 6. Data Subject Rights (Under Qatari Law No. 13 of 2016)

Under **Law No. 13 of 2016 on the Protection of Personal Data**, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten):** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations. We will respond within **30 days** of a valid request.
- **Right to Restriction of Processing:** Request that we limit the processing of your data.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) to **privacy@saqynrabt.com** or in writing to our registered address. We will respond to your request within **30 days**, free of charge. We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing your request.

If you are a resident of the European Union or the European Economic Area, you additionally have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Supervisory Authority under the GDPR.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your data is transferred to and stored on servers located in the **United States** (NeonDB, Cloudflare R2, Upstash Redis, Pinecone) and **Europe**.

Under **Law No. 13 of 2016**, international transfers of personal data are permissible provided that appropriate safeguards are in place. We rely on the following mechanisms:

- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs):** As approved by the European Commission for EU/EEA data subjects.
- **Data Processing Agreements:** We maintain DPAs with all sub-processors that provide the same level of data protection as required under Qatari law.
- **Adequate Protection:** The receiving countries maintain data protection regimes that we have assessed as adequate for the purposes of this transfer.

## 8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, as required under **Articles 21–23 of Law No. 13 of 2016** and **Law No. 14 of 2014 on Cybercrime**. These measures include:

- Encryption of data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Access controls, multi-factor authentication, and role-based permissions
- Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments
- Audit logging of all administrative access
- Incident response procedures (see our Security Document for details)

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following categories of cookies:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for authentication (via Clerk) and security purposes. These cookies are necessary for the dashboard to function properly.
- **Analytics Cookies:** We use Cloudflare Insights and Vercel Analytics for basic traffic and performance monitoring.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that **disabling essential cookies may break dashboard functionality** and prevent you from accessing your account.
