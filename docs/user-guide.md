# SAQYN RABT — User Guide & Manual

Welcome to the SAQYN RABT Private AI operations console.

## 1. Getting Started
To activate your B2B workspace:
1. Log in via Clerk auth on [saqynrabt.com](https://saqynrabt.com)
2. Add your company profile and select a plan
3. Access your secure dashboard route at `/dashboard`

## 2. RAG Staff Assistant (Chatbot)
Upload PDF documents containing your hotel, clinic, or shop SOPs to the **Documents** section. The system automatically segments text chunks to train your private RAG chatbot instance.

## 3. Front-Desk Automation (Guest Queue)
Manage customer/guest calls and requests automatically via AI voice agent queues. Review pending assignments under `/dashboard/automation`.
