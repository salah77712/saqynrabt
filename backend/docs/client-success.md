# SAQYN RABT — Client Success Operations Manual

## 1. Automated Health Index Score
Client account risk matrices are calculated daily based on active consumption and CSAT indicators:
- **Green (Healthy, 80-100)**: Steady daily API requests, positive CSAT feedback, and regular dashboard usage.
- **Yellow (Moderate, 50-79)**: Reduced query volumes or open support tickets.
- **Red (Critical, 0-49)**: Low login frequencies. CS success team is notified immediately to schedule QBR reviews.

## 2. QBR Templates
Every 90 days, the success team compiles performance review summaries displaying customer savings and AI accuracy indexes.
