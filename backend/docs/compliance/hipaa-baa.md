# HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA) — SAQYN RABT

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into by SAQYN RABT and the client designated as a Covered Entity under HIPAA regulations.

## 1. Protected Health Information (PHI)
* SAQYN RABT agrees to safeguard all Protected Health Information (PHI) stored in company knowledge bases.
* Dedicated database schemas are provisioned for Covered Entities to guarantee physical isolation.
* Data is encrypted at rest using client-specific keys managed via AWS KMS / Cloudflare Key Management.

## 2. Permitted Uses and Disclosures
* The Business Associate shall use PHI solely to operate the RAG AI chatbot and queue automation dispatches for the Covered Entity's workspace.
* Employee access logs record all document retrieval events with timestamps.
