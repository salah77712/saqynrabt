# SAQYN RABT — AI Governance & Accountability Policy

## 1. Objective & Purpose
This document logs the ethical governance framework ensuring safe, compliant, and non-biased model outputs across all client deployments.

## 2. Acceptable Model Conduct
- **Data Subject Privacy**: Corporate knowledge files uploaded to R2 are strictly scoped and never pooled for base model training.
- **Toxicity & Harm Avoidance**: Input guardrail filters discard query chains prompting emergency routing actions unless verified by business parameters.
- **Explainability**: Decision logs tracking confidence indexes are archived for 90 days.
